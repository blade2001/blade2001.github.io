About Me

Hello! My name is Shebin G and I enjoy creating things that live on the internet. My interest in web development started back in 2008 when I decided to try editing custom Tumblr themes — turns out hacking together HTML & CSS taught me a lot about architecture & design!

Fast-forward to today, and I've had the privilege of working at an advertising agency, a start-up, a huge corporation, and a student-led design studio. My main focus these days is building accessible, inclusive products and digital experiences.

When I'm not at my computer, I'm usually outside running a local 10 km trail, exploring new tech shops, or reading a good sci-fi novel or AI, Tech or Science blogs.
